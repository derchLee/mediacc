MediaCC Icon Generator

Generate PNG icons from SVG for different sizes

Instructions:

  1. Click "Generate All Icons" to create previews
  2. Click "Download All Icons" to download all icon files
  3. Place the downloaded files in the public folder:
    • favicon.ico (16x16, 32x32, 48x48 combined)
    • apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)
    • icon-192.png (192x192)
    • icon-512.png (512x512)
